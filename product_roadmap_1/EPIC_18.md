# Basic onboarding flow
**Epic ID:** GF-58 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P1 — High | **Swim Lane:** Collaboration & Admin | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Guided first-session experience that gets new users to their first 'aha moment' as fast as possible. Steps: select your service type, define your territory (or accept a default), view your first properties on the map, explore a property detail page, and reveal your first contact. The goal is time-to-value under 10 minutes — a new rep should be able to find a real prospect they want to call within their first session. Without this, users drop off before discovering the product's value.

**Success Criteria:** 80%+ of new users complete the onboarding flow. Time from signup to first contact reveal is under 10 minutes. Users who complete onboarding have 3x higher 7-day retention than those who skip it. Onboarding can be skipped by experienced users.
