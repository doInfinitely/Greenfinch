# Territory definitions
**Epic ID:** GF-8 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P1 — High | **Swim Lane:** Prospecting & Search | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Allow org admins to define geographic territories assigned to individual reps. Territories can be defined by zip code, county, or drawn boundaries on the map. All prospecting views filter by territory so reps focus on their area. Overlap detection warns when territories conflict. This is essential for team-based selling — without it, reps waste time on properties outside their area or duplicate effort with teammates.

**Success Criteria:** Admins can create and assign territories in under 5 minutes. Reps see only their territory by default. Overlap warnings fire correctly. Territory boundaries visible on map view.

**Comment (Remy):** Users/admins can define geographic territories (by zip, county, or drawn boundary) assigned to reps. Filter views by territory. Prevent overlap warnings.
