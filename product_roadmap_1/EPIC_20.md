# Basic metric reporting
**Epic ID:** GF-64 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P2 — Medium | **Swim Lane:** Reporting & Export | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Dashboard showing key product usage and pipeline metrics: properties searched, contacts revealed, pipeline activity (opportunities created, moved, won, lost), credit usage patterns, and active user counts. Per-user and per-org views. Helps org admins understand ROI and helps Greenfinch internally understand product health and engagement patterns. This data also informs pricing optimization and feature prioritization.

**Success Criteria:** Dashboard shows at least 8 key metrics covering usage, pipeline, and credits. Data refreshes at least daily. Per-user breakdown available for org admins. Greenfinch internal view shows cross-org aggregate metrics. Metrics are exportable.
