# Basic revenue estimation
**Epic ID:** GF-24 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P1 — High | **Swim Lane:** Account Intelligence | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Rule-based estimation of annual contract value by service type for each property. For landscaping: uses lot size, permeable area percentage, property category, and building class to estimate what a typical annual landscaping contract would be worth. Displayed prominently on property detail pages and as a sortable column in list views. This is a key differentiator — no competitor tells a service provider 'this property is worth ~$45K/year in landscaping revenue to you'. Starts with landscaping, extends to other segments as rules are defined.

**Success Criteria:** Revenue estimates displayed for 90%+ of commercial properties in landscaping. Estimates are within 30% of actual contract values (validated against pilot customer data). Users report that estimates are directionally useful for prioritization. Revenue estimate is sortable/filterable in list view.

**Comment (Remy):** Rule-based estimated annual contract value by service type (landscaping first). Based on lot size, building sqft, permeable area, property use, category. Displayed on property detail and in list views.
