# Billing admin — usage monitoring
**Epic ID:** GF-47 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P1 — High | **Swim Lane:** Billing & Monetization | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Dashboard for org admins to monitor their team's credit usage, view billing history, see current plan details, and understand spending patterns. Shows who on the team is using credits, on what actions, and how quickly they're consuming their allocation. Helps admins forecast whether they need to upgrade their plan or purchase additional credits before running out.

**Success Criteria:** Admin can see total credits used, remaining, and rollover balance. Usage broken down by user and by action type. Billing history shows all invoices and payments. Usage trend chart shows consumption rate. Admin receives warning when credits are 80% consumed.

**Comment (Remy):** Org admins can view credit usage, billing history, and current plan details.
