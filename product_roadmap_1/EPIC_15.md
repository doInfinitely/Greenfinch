# Rollover credit system
**Epic ID:** GF-46 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P0 — Critical | **Swim Lane:** Billing & Monetization | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Monthly credit allocation by pricing tier that users spend on high-value actions: revealing contact details, accessing premium property data, using AI features, and requesting deep research. Unused credits roll over month-to-month with a configurable cap (e.g., 2x monthly allocation). Credits initially shareable across all org users. Users can purchase additional credit packs for burst usage. Credits are the core usage-based monetization layer — they align Greenfinch revenue with the value users extract and create a natural expansion mechanism as teams use the product more.

**Success Criteria:** Credits deducted correctly for each gated action. Credit balance visible to users at all times. Rollover logic works correctly with cap enforcement. Additional credit packs purchasable via self-service for admin accounts. Credit usage analytics available to org admins. Users understand what costs credits and how many they have.
