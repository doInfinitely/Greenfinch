# Segments: Tree trimming, irrigation
**Epic ID:** GF-80 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P1 — High | **Swim Lane:** Segments & Geography | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Expand supported service segments to include tree trimming and irrigation alongside landscaping. These are the most closely adjacent segments — often sold by the same companies or by companies in the same industry network. Validates that the product's property intelligence is valuable beyond landscaping specifically. May require minor adjustments to revenue estimation models and filter defaults but should not require significant product changes.

**Success Criteria:** At least 2 customers from tree trimming or irrigation segments onboarded. Revenue estimation adapted for these segments (different pricing models than full-service landscaping). Segment-specific filter defaults available. Customer feedback confirms the product is valuable for these segments without major gaps.

**Comment (Remy):** Expand service segment support to include tree trimming and irrigation. Validate that property classification and revenue estimation models extend to these segments.
