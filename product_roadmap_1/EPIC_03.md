# Manager role
**Epic ID:** GF-57 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P2 — Medium | **Swim Lane:** Collaboration & Admin | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Intermediate role between Admin and Member for team leads and sales managers. Managers can view all team members' activity, assign territories, see pipeline for their direct reports, and access team-level analytics — but cannot manage billing, org settings, or add/remove members. This supports the org structure of a mid-size service company where a sales manager oversees 5-10 reps.

**Success Criteria:** Manager role has clearly differentiated permissions from Admin and Member. Managers see all pipeline and activity data for their team. Managers can assign territories and redistribute leads. Managers cannot access billing or make org-level changes.
