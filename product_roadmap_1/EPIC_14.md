# Residential property evaluation
**Epic ID:** GF-82 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P2 — Medium | **Swim Lane:** Segments & Geography | **Owner:** Remy, Cory Oliver | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Decision point: evaluate whether to add residential properties based on market signal from pilot customers. Some landscaping and property service companies serve both commercial and residential — if we hear consistent demand, adding residential unlocks a larger addressable market. However, residential has a different decision-maker structure (homeowners/HOAs vs. PMs) and may require different enrichment approaches. This epic is about making an informed decision, not committing to build.

**Success Criteria:** At least 5 pilot customers interviewed about residential demand. Decision documented with clear rationale (build, defer, or decline). If proceeding: scope of residential MVP defined. If deferring: criteria for revisiting documented.
