# Chatbot with human escalation
**Epic ID:** GF-71 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P2 — Medium | **Swim Lane:** Infrastructure | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** In-app support chat that provides AI-powered first-line response for common questions (how to use features, credit balance inquiries, data quality questions) with escalation to human support for complex issues. Reduces support burden on founders and gives users instant help. AI responses are grounded in product documentation and account data. Escalation routes to email or Slack for human follow-up.

**Success Criteria:** AI resolves 60%+ of support inquiries without human escalation. Average response time under 5 seconds for AI, under 4 hours for human escalation. Users rate AI responses as helpful 70%+ of the time. Support chat is accessible from every page in the app.
