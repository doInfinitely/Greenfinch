# Customer status — manual flagging
**Epic ID:** GF-10 | **Phase:** Phase II: Expanded Pilot | **Priority:** P1 — High | **Swim Lane:** Prospecting & Search | **Owner:** Remy | **Build Status:** NEW

**Description:** Expand the basic customer status tracking into richer, team-visible flagging. Users can mark properties as 'existing customer', 'competitor serviced' (with competitor name), 'do not contact', 'hot lead', etc. Flags include optional notes and are visible to all team members. This builds institutional knowledge about the territory — a new rep joining the team can immediately see which properties are spoken for and which are open.

**Success Criteria:** At least 5 distinct flag types available. Flags are visible on map markers and list rows without clicking into the property. Notes are searchable. Team members can filter views by flag type.
